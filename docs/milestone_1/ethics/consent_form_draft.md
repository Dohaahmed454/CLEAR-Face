# Consent Form Draft 
 
## Study title 
 
CLEAR-Face: Risk-Aware Physical Evidence Reacquisition for Facial-Expression Recognition 
 
## What participants will do 
 
Participants may be asked to face a camera, adjust lighting, move closer, hold still, or provide a short sequence so the system can test whether a better visual observation improves reliability. 
 
## Voluntary participation 
 
Participation is voluntary. Participants may stop at any time. 
 
## Data privacy 
 
Collected data will be stored using anonymous participant IDs. Real face data will not be publicly released unless explicitly permitted by ethics approval and consent conditions. 
 
## Withdrawal and deletion 
 
Participants may request deletion of their data according to the approved retention and deletion policy.
