Final Contribution Statement

This project makes four focused contributions:

CARE: a learned controller for selecting prediction, targeted physical evidence reacquisition, short-sequence capture, or abstention under prediction risk, user burden, latency, and repeated-failure cost.
CLEAR-Face-R: a paired synthetic-and-real benchmark linking unreliable observations, requested actions, new observations, outcomes, compliance, burden, and time.
Multi-evidence justification: a structured explanation layer combining Action Units, landmarks, attribution, perturbation, and temporal evidence.
Integrated validation: matched-risk and matched-burden evaluation, synthetic-to-real transfer, unseen participant/device testing, ablations, XAI faithfulness, and explanation grounding.
