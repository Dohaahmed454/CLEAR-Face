# Milestone 1 Summary 
 
Milestone 1 freezes the research scope, novelty claim, dataset strategy, split plan, CLEAR-Face-R schema, baseline policies, primary metrics, minimum-success thresholds, and ethics preparation. 
 
## Deliverables completed 
 - Novelty comparison matrix. 
 - Final contribution statement. 
 - Research questions and hypotheses. 
 - Architecture and non-goal summary. 
 - Dataset selection plan. 
 - Label mapping plan. 
 - Participant-disjoint split plan. 
 - CLEAR-Face-R schema. 
 - Baseline policy definitions. 
 - Primary metrics. 
 - Minimum-success thresholds. 
 - Experimental protocol. 
 - Ethics, consent, privacy, retention, and deletion drafts. 
 
## Exit-gate status 
 
Proceed to Milestone 2 only after: 
 
1. The novelty claim is defensible. 
2. The dataset and split protocol prevent leakage. 
3. The CLEAR-Face-R schema is complete enough for synthetic records. 
4. The real-trial ethics documents are approved or formally submitted.
