# Primary Metrics 
 
## Decision and CARE metrics 
 - Selective risk. 
 - Coverage. 
 - Recovery rate. 
 - Post-action error. 
 - Risk reduction. 
 - Abstention rate. 
 - Action-selection confusion. 
 - Unnecessary prompts. 
 - Repeated failures. 
 - Prompts per resolved case. 
 - Time to decision. 
 - Burden-adjusted utility. 
 
## Transfer and generalization metrics 
 - Synthetic-to-real drop. 
 - Unseen-participant performance. 
 - Unseen-device/environment performance. 
 - Learning curves for limited real adaptation. 
 
## XAI metrics 
 - Deletion/insertion or occlusion effects. 
 - Facial-versus-background attribution concentration. 
 - Stability under small transformations. 
 - Cross-method agreement. 
 - Temporal consistency where available. 
 - Pre/post evidence improvement. 
 
## Language metrics 
 - Supported-statement rate. 
 - Unsupported-detail rate. 
 - Contradiction rate. 
 - Decision-explanation consistency. 
 - Correct limitation reporting. 
 - Prohibited-inference rate. 
 - Readability. 
 - Inter-rater agreement. 
 
## Efficiency metrics 
 - Mean latency. 
 - Median latency. 
 - p95 latency. 
 - Memory. 
 - Model size. 
 - Frames per second. 
 - Offline operation.
