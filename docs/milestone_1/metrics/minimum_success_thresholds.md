# Minimum-Success Thresholds 
 
Milestone 2 may proceed only if: 
 
1. Each selected synthetic failure condition reliably harms FER performance. 
2. The intended correction produces measurable improvement. 
3. Synthetic pre/post pairs are reproducible. 
4. Quality measurements distinguish clean and degraded images. 
 
Milestone 3 may proceed to real-data preparation only if: 
 
1. Synthetic CARE beats at least one strong baseline under matched burden, matched risk, or equal interaction budget. 
2. Any accuracy gain is not only caused by asking for more prompts. 
3. CARE ablations show which feature groups contribute to performance. 
4. The frozen synthetic CARE model can be exported for later transfer testing. 
 
Milestone 5 final success requires: 
 
1. Real-adapted CARE improves over fixed rules and uncertainty-only selection, or achieves equivalent risk with lower burden/latency. 
2. Limited real adaptation improves over frozen synthetic CARE. 
3. The evaluation remains valid on unseen participants and, where possible, unseen devices/environments.
