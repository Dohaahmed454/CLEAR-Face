Explicit Non-Goals

This project does not target:

Identity recognition.
Age prediction.
Gender classification.
General facial attribute recognition.
Inference of true internal emotions.
Personality, intent, deception, or mental-health inference.
A new conformal-prediction theory.
A new foundational attribution method.
A large language model trained from scratch.
A full atomic-claim verification framework.
A large human trust experiment.
A standalone fairness-mitigation contribution.
Mandatory mobile or edge deployment.
A large FER backbone leaderboard.
